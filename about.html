About Us

About Us

The vision for Our Hotel Anand Renaissance began back in 1995 when our founder Late Sh. Raj Kumar decided to deliver excellent hospitality and remarkable service in our city with a strong history - FEROZEPUR. In early 1995 we began with the first banquet of our hometown by the name of ANAND MARRIAGE PALACE, was able to set itself apart by focusing on personalised and quality services. Our team is mostly involved with tasks like setting up, serving and catering activities. We have more than 24 years of hospitality experience. With that experience, our team has grown to become one of the most trusted hospitality provider in the city. In 2016, we gave a new look to our business with an extensive renovation to the building and changed the trading name to 'HOTEL ANAND RENAISSANCE'. The new look consists of 2 luxurious Banquets namely UTSAV for grand celebrations and get togethers having the capacity of 250-300 guests under one roof, a Kitty Hall/Conference Hall named as RAUNAK for small gatherings and multi-purposes holding upto 50-70 guests. After a long successful era under Mrs.Teena Kakkar and Mr. Tarsem Kakkar, their son Dhruv Kakkar has been managing operations since 2016.

Hotel Management

Dhruv Kakkar (General Manager)

He holds a Bachelor’s degree in Hotel and Hospitality Management from Panjab University, Chandigarh. He was the Gold Medalist of his batch and was also awarded with the Student of the batch. As it was in his blood to be a hospitality professional, he has always done an outstanding job in climbing the ladder of success alongwith his parents. After graduating in year 2016, he set foot in the family business and with his earnest tacts, resourcefulness and hard work, combined with his blood, sweat and tears has led us to serve remarkably and earn self esteem in the city.

Teena Kakkar (Director)

She is a self-taught graduated business woman with a capability to face and overcome the ups and downs of the company.

Tarsem Kakkar (Director)

Our strong Management Team of three, practices the simplest and extraordinary philosophy that was inherited by our founder Late Shri. Raj Kumar to his son Mr. Tarsem Kakkar and further he passed it on to his son Mr. Dhruv Kakkar that says, “Atithi Devo bhava” which prescribes a dynamic of the host–guest relationship. Mr. Tarsem Kakkar, has been the driving force of our hotel since he was 18. His constant hard work and concentrated efforts and years of professional experience in the industry led us to reach the peaks of success.
