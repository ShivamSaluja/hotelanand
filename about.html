About Us

The vision for Our Hotel Anand Renaissance began back in the 1990's when our founder Late Sh. Raj Kumar decided to deliver excellent hospitality and remarkable service in our city with the strong history- FEROZEPUR. In early 1990â€™s we began with our first business of tent rentals which is still run by the heirs of the founder, named as RAJ TENT HOUSE. The first banquet of our hometown by the name ANAND PALACE was able to set itself apart by focusing on personalised and quality services. Subsequently in 2002, we introduced the first open banquet in the city called ANAND BANQUET HALL. Our team is mostly involved with tasks like setting up, serving and catering activities for our guests. Furthermore, in 2006 we added another feather in our hat by renovating our banquet into the first fully air-conditioned banquet in the city. We have more than 24 years of hospitality experience. With that experience, our Team has grown to become one of the most trusted hotel in the city. In 2016, we invigorated our banquet into a hotel with various facilites such as a banquet Hall named as UTSAV for grand celebrations and get togethers having the capacity of 250-300 guests under one roof, a Kitty Hall/Conference Hall named as RAUNAK for small gatherings and multi-purposes holding upto 50-70 guests atmost. Furthermore, we added up 8 different hotel rooms with different services including - 2 Suites, 3 Super Deluxe and 3 Deluxe rooms for luxurious and comfortable stays which are fully furnished and 24 hours services available by our team. This upgradation led us to update the name to HOTEL ANAND RENAISSANCE. The business is currently run by Mrs. Teena Kakkar CEO,Mr. Tarsem Kumar CEO and Mr Dhruv Kakkar General Manager. It is a perfect father-son duo who have been able to further the legacy started by their predecessors. By working hard in the past years, we have earned good respect and esteem in the city.
